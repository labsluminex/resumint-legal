Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy matters to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how ResuMint handles your information. We've designed our app with privacy in mind — your resume data stays on your device.

⚡
The Short Version
  • Your resume data stays on your device — we never see it.
  • We don't have user accounts or cloud sync.
  • Ads & analytics use anonymous data only.
  • You're always in control — uninstall to delete everything.
🗄️
1. Information We Collect
In short: We don't collect your resume data. Everything stays on your phone.
ResuMint is designed as an offline-first app. Your resume content — including your name, email, work history, education, skills, and any other information you enter — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive database). We do not transmit, upload, or store this information on any remote server. We cannot access, view, or recover your resumes.
📱
2. How Your Data is Stored
Your data lives on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes it.
All resume data is saved locally in your device's app storage using an encrypted local database (Hive). This data: • Never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share a PDF • Is not backed up to our servers • Is permanently deleted if you uninstall the app • Cannot be recovered by us if lost We strongly recommend exporting and backing up your resumes regularly using the Save PDF feature.
📦
3. Third-Party Services
We use Google services to keep the app running and free.
ResuMint integrates the following third-party services: • Google AdMob — Displays ads to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com. • Google Play Billing — Processes in-app purchases for Premium subscriptions. Payment information is handled entirely by Google; we never see your card details. • Firebase Analytics — Helps us understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which features are most used) to improve the experience. Data is anonymized and aggregated. • Firebase Crashlytics — Collects crash reports to help us fix bugs faster. Reports contain technical information about the crash but no personal resume data. These services may collect anonymous device information such as device model, OS version, and app usage patterns. They do not access your resume content.
🛡️
4. Permissions We Request
We only ask for what we need to save your PDFs.
ResuMint may request the following device permissions: • Storage — Required to save your exported resume PDFs to your Downloads folder. On Android 11 and above, this is handled automatically without a permission prompt. • Internet — Required for ads, in-app purchases, and analytics. We do not request access to your contacts, camera, location, microphone, or any other sensitive permissions.
🔒
5. Your Rights
You're always in control of your data.
Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can at any time: • View, edit, or delete any resume directly in the app • Export your resumes as PDFs to keep your own backups • Uninstall the app to permanently delete all stored data • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA regulations including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we may hold. Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are exercised directly on your device.
👥
6. Children's Privacy
ResuMint is intended for adults building careers.
ResuMint is designed for users aged 13 and above who are building resumes for educational, professional, or career purposes. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will assist where possible.
🔐
7. Data Security
Local-first means safer by default.
Because your resume data never leaves your device, it is inherently more secure than cloud-based alternatives. There is no server we operate that could be breached to expose your information. For data handled by third-party services (AdMob, Firebase, Google Play Billing), we rely on Google's industry-standard security measures.
📄
8. Changes to This Policy
We'll notify you if anything important changes.
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new features, third-party integrations, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and may notify you within the app. Continued use of ResuMint after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
